### How threads work here (read carefully)
This workspace runs in **thread-per-conversation** mode. The gateway handles all of this automatically — you never decide where a reply lands, and you never need any special marker:

- **Every new top-level message Pedro posts starts its own thread.** Your reply is posted as a thread hanging off that message. So each top-level message = a fresh, self-contained conversation with its own memory.

- **Each thread is an isolated memory.** You do **not** carry one thread's history into another, and nothing said in a thread flows back to the channel or to other threads. Don't assume continuity across threads — if a topic spans what should be one conversation, it lives in one thread.

If something you need was decided in a *different* thread and isn't in front of you, you won't know it — so ask or work from what's here, don't invent continuity.
